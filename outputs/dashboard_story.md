## Dashboard Story

### Executive Summary

The dashboard gives an overall view of sales performance across regions, products, shipping, discounts, and returns. Sales are strong overall, but profit can be improved by reducing returns, reviewing discount strategies, and improving delivery performance.


### What is Performing Well

Sales remain strong throughout the period, with an increase in the most recent months. The South region has the highest sales among all regions, showing that it is the best-performing region. Some product sub-categories also generate higher profits than others.


### What is Underperforming

Not all regions perform equally well, with some regions generating lower sales than the South region. The discount analysis shows that offering higher discounts does not always result in higher profits. Some products also generate lower profits than others.


### What Risks Are Visible

The overall return rate is **6.55%**, and the Furniture category has a higher return rate than Technology. Longer delivery times for some shipping modes may also affect customer satisfaction and lead to more returns.


### What Opportunities Are Visible

The business can improve by using the successful sales approach from the South region in other regions. Reviewing discount policies, improving delivery times, and reducing product returns can help increase profit and improve customer satisfaction.


### Recommended Business Actions

* Study why the South region performs better and apply similar strategies in other regions.
* Review discount levels to ensure they improve sales without reducing profit.
* Investigate the reasons for higher Furniture returns and take corrective action.
* Improve shipping methods that have longer delivery times.
* Continue tracking sales, profit, and return rates to measure business performance.


### Limitations of the Dashboard

The dashboard shows overall business performance but does not explain the reasons behind the results. It does not include information such as marketing activities, inventory levels, customer feedback, or competitor data. More detailed analysis is needed to understand the root causes of performance differences.


### Suggested Next Analysis

* Compare profit across all product categories and sub-categories.
* Find the discount range that gives the best profit.
* Compare sales, profit, and returns across customer segments.
* Analyse why Furniture products have higher return rates.
* Study delivery performance by region and shipping mode.
* Analyse monthly sales by region and product category to identify seasonal trends.
